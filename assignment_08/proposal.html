Final Project Proposal

Topic

  • The website will explore the history and cultural significance of jazz dance and music, with a focus on how jazz connects to cultural identity, racism, and liberation.

    • Audience

  • The intended audience for this website includes students and enthusiasts of jazz music and dance, as well as those interested in the cultural and historical aspects of this art form.

    • Design & Style

    • Color Scheme: The primary color will be blue to evoke the mood of jazz music. The secondary color will be golden yellow (#FFD700) for a touch of classic jazz era elegance.
    • Design Inspiration:
      • Jazz Radio
      • All About Jazz
      • What is Jazz

    Scope

    • "Jazz Through the Ages": Exploring the history and evolution of jazz, with interactive timelines and multimedia elements.
    • "Cultural Reflections": Analyzing jazz's role in cultural identity, its stance on issues like racism, and its liberating influence on society.
    • "Jazz Today": Discussing jazz's modern impact in movies, clubs, and contemporary artists, using interactive elements to engage users.

    Extras

    • Event-driven DOM Manipulation: Users can interact with timelines, audio clips, and jazz-related quizzes.
    • JSON API Integration: We will connect to an API to provide users with real-time information about their favorite jazz musicians and albums.
    • DOM Storage: Local storage will allow users to save their progress and preferences for a personalized experience.
    • React Library: To create dynamic components that enhance user interaction.
